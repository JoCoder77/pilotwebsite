Why Coral Sky Aviation

Building on over 20 years of flight training history at Rand Airport, Germiston.

Our Legacy

A Proud Training Heritage

Coral Sky Aviation operates from the established general aviation training facilities at Rand Airport, Germiston. Over the past two decades, this facility has produced more than 400 Commercial Pilot License graduates who now fly with domestic and international airline fleets globally.

Our curriculum continues this proud legacy by providing flight training, cabin crew service, and aircraft maintenance courses aligned with the latest South African Civil Aviation Authority standards. We focus on training excellence, structured flight hours, and comprehensive ground instruction led by active airline captain mentors.

Flight Horizons

Inspiring Training Environments

Training at Coral Sky Aviation extends far beyond the local airfield circuit. Our flight syllabus incorporates cross-country navigation routes that expose cadets to diverse geographical terrains, challenging weather patterns, and mountain navigation corridors.

Advanced Airspace Handling

Learn complex radio procedures and controlled airspace transitions in a highly active general aviation environment.

Cross-Country Navigation

Fly visual and instrument navigation legs across beautiful South African mountain passes and coastlines, building robust command decision skills.
