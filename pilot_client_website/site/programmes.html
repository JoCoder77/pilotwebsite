How to Begin Your Training

1. Submit Enquiry

Submit an enquiry via Email or WhatsApp. Our General Manager and administrative team respond within 24 hours with a personalized quotation calculated in your local currency.

2. Interview & Campus Tour

Local walk-in students are invited to tour our training facilities at Rand Airport. International cadets will be scheduled for a detailed WhatsApp video call consultation.

3. Secure Your Slot

Complete registration and class medical clearance to secure your slot. Ground school and flight training cohorts launch regularly.
